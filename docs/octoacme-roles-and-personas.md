# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
The QA Lead owns the overall quality strategy, test planning, and final signoff for releases. They work closely with developers and Product Managers to define acceptance criteria, identify risk areas, and ensure that shipped features meet quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain the overall test strategy and quality standards
- Plan, coordinate, and oversee manual and automated test activities
- Collaborate with developers on acceptance criteria and testability
- Own defect triage and escalation processes
- Provide final quality signoff before releases
- Track quality metrics (defect rates, test coverage, escape rates)

### Goals
- Prevent regressions and reduce defect escape rates
- Align quality expectations across engineering and product
- Enable faster, safer releases through automation and clear standards

### Typical Communication
- Participates in sprint planning and backlog refinement to validate acceptance criteria with **Product Managers**
- Coordinates closely with **Developers** on test coverage, bug prioritization, and fix verification
- Reports quality status and release readiness to **Project Managers**
- Works with **Release Managers** to ensure release gates and rollback criteria are defined

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
The UX Designer is responsible for understanding user needs and translating them into intuitive workflows, wireframes, and prototypes. They validate usability with stakeholders and end-users, ensuring that the product is both functional and delightful to use.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing
- Design and iterate on user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Collaborate with Product Managers to align designs with product goals
- Validate designs with end-users and incorporate feedback
- Maintain a design system and ensure visual consistency
- Communicate design rationale and trade-offs to engineering

### Goals
- Deliver experiences that meet user needs and reduce friction
- Reduce rework by validating designs before implementation
- Ensure accessibility and inclusivity across all user interfaces

### Typical Communication
- Partners closely with **Product Managers** to align on requirements and prioritization
- Shares wireframes and prototypes with **Developers** for technical feasibility feedback
- Presents designs to stakeholders and incorporates feedback iteratively
- Works with **QA Leads** to clarify expected user interactions and edge cases

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
The Technical Writer maintains living documentation, release notes, and onboarding materials to ensure institutional knowledge is accurate, accessible, and up to date. They work cross-functionally to surface knowledge gaps and make complex information easy to understand.

### Responsibilities
- Author and maintain process documents, API references, user guides, and onboarding materials
- Write and publish release notes in collaboration with engineering and product
- Identify and fill documentation gaps through cross-functional collaboration
- Ensure documentation follows consistent structure and style standards
- Manage documentation tooling and versioning

### Goals
- Reduce onboarding time for new team members and users
- Ensure documentation stays current with product and process changes
- Surface knowledge gaps before they impact delivery or user experience

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with **Developers** to document technical specifications and API changes
- Partners with **Product Managers** to document feature requirements and release notes
- Works with **Project Managers** to keep process documentation aligned with actual workflows
- Coordinates with **QA Leads** to capture test plans and quality processes

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
The Release Manager plans, coordinates, and communicates all aspects of the release process. They ensure that transitions between development, testing, and production environments are smooth, well-communicated, and supported by clear rollback plans.

### Responsibilities
- Own the release calendar and coordinate release activities across teams
- Define and enforce release readiness criteria and deployment checklists
- Communicate release timelines, scope, and risks to stakeholders
- Coordinate cutover activities between Dev, Test, and Prod environments
- Maintain rollback and incident response plans for each release
- Conduct post-release retrospectives and track release metrics

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, well-communicated releases on schedule
- Minimize deployment risk through preparation, coordination, and clear rollback procedures
- Drive continuous improvement of the release process

### Typical Communication
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** to align release windows with project milestones
- Works with **Developers** and **QA Leads** to confirm release readiness and resolve blockers
- Communicates release plans, changes, and outcomes to stakeholders and leadership
- Partners with **Technical Writers** to ensure release notes are accurate and published on time

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
The Business Analyst gathers and documents requirements, validates that proposed solutions align with business goals, and supports the Product Manager with data-driven insights. They bridge the gap between business stakeholders and the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit, document, and validate business and functional requirements
- Analyze current processes and identify opportunities for improvement
- Create use cases, user stories, and process flow diagrams
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business objectives
- Support the Product Manager with data analysis and reporting
- Facilitate requirements workshops and stakeholder reviews

### Goals
- Ensure delivered solutions solve the right business problems
- Reduce ambiguity in requirements to minimize rework
- Provide data-driven insights to support prioritization and decision-making

### Typical Communication
- Works closely with **Product Managers** to refine and prioritize requirements based on business value
- Collaborates with **Developers** to clarify requirements and resolve ambiguities during implementation
- Engages stakeholders and subject matter experts to validate requirements and acceptance criteria
- Partners with **Project Managers** to track requirement coverage and flag scope changes
